## Visual cautions

- Do not show the player as Moses.
- Do not make golden calf scenes attractive or triumphant.
- Do not turn Sinai into generic fantasy magic; use biblical awe, cloud, fire, trumpet, distance, and boundary.
- Keep the witness perspective below the mountain.
- Avoid gore or explicit violence in judgment scenes.
